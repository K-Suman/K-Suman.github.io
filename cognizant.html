Mention below the Steps followed by during Cognizant Hiring Process 2022:-
  • Round 1: Qualifier Round- This round is the elimination round . This round is the common round for GenC and GenC Next .
  • Round 2: GenC Next-This round is also called GenC Next. As if you clear this round cut-off the you get a chance to appear for GenC Next Profile Interview.
  • Round 3: Interview- Technical Interview and HR Interview.

Cognizant Off Campus Placement Test Patter Overview :-
Round 1

Folloring are the scetions that are asked in Cognizant Placement Exam Round 1:

  • Quants
  • Logical
  • English
  • Code Debugging (Automata Fix)

Note : After clearing Round 1 if you score good marks you will be eligible for Round 2 Test which is termed as GenC Next

and if you didn't score good marks then you will be allowed to attend Round 2 Interview termed as GenC.

Round 2

In Round 2 you will be provided with 2 sections:

  • Section 1-Programming Skills
  • Section 2-Problem Solving Data Structures and Algorithm Capabilities.

Note :If you will clear Round 2 you will be eligible to give an Interview for GenC Next otherwise GenC Profile.

GenC Interview

In this Interview ROund you will be asked questions on Technical Cognitive Abilities and Right Attitude. If you manage to clear this round you will get Cognizant Offer Letter.

  • Salary During Probation - 4LPA
  • Salary After Probation - 4.5 LPA
Round 3

This round is basically GenC Next Interview Round. You will be asked questions on the topics:

  • Problem Articulation
  • Algorithm Capabilities
  • Programming Concepts
  • Cognitive Abilities
  • Right Attitude

After clearing Round 3 that is GenC Next Interview Round, package will be 6.5 LPA.

Important Note!
  1. No Negative Marking
  2. Cognizant Off-Campus is Adaptive
