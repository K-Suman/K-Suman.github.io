What is TCS NQT ?

TCS NQT stands for TCS National Qualifier Test. TCS NQT is a multi-level assessment to access the following competencies and skills:

  1. Competence on the core cognitive processes required for entry-level jobs.
  2. Industry-specific knowledge and insights.
  3. TCS NQT has recently updated its pattern.Here you will get all of the most up-to-date information about TCS NQT Hiring Process and TCS NQT Exam Pattern.

Mention below the Steps followed by during TCS NQT Hiring Process 2022:-

1.Online Written Test

2.Technical Interview

3.HR Interview

Detailed Hiring Process of TCS NQT for Freshers:-
Writing Test

  • TCS NQT Written Round is the first section of Hiring Process.
  • It is an online written test, which is regarded as one of the most difficult rounds in the entire hiring process.
  • Aptitude , Logical Reasoning , Verbal Reasoning , CMCQ and Coding these are the sections of the wriiten test.

Technical Interview

  • This is the most difficult stage of the hiring process, so prepare as much as you can for this round.
  • Your technical knowledge , biography , and main project will be the focus of the whole interview process.
  • The major goal of this round is to assess the student's technical knowledge.
  • There is a chance you will be given a piece of code to write.

HR and MR Round

  • This is the simplest round in the whole TCS NQT 2022 Hiring Process.
  • In this round , interviewer will mostly test your confidence and leadership quality.
  • They will mostly describe the Job Profile, Job Salary , and relocations in this round.

Important Note!
  1. No Negative Marking
  2. TCS NQT is Adaptive
