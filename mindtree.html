Latest Pattern for the MindTree Recruitment Process for Freshers is given below-:-

MindTree Recruitment Process has different Rounds of Selection Process:

  • Round 1 - Online Aptitude Exam.
  • Round 2 - Spoken English or Grammer Round.
  • Technical Interview
  • HR Interview

MindTree Off Campus Placement Test Patter Overview :-
Round 1 : Written Test

Folloring are the sections that are asked in MindTree Placement Exam Round 1:

  • Quants
  • Logical
  • Verbal
  • Coding Test

Note : After clearing Round 1 if you score good marks you will be eligible for Round 2 Test .

Round 2 : Spoken English and Grammar Test
  • Spoken English
  • 1 Mins Topic Speaking
  • Basic Grammar
  • Audio Comprehension

Note :If you will clear Round 2 you will be eligible to give an Interview.

Technical Interview
  • This is the most difficult stage of the hiring process, so prepare as much as you can for this round.
  • Your technical knowledge , biography , and main project will be the focus of the whole interview process.
  • The major goal of this round is to assess the student's technical knowledge.
  • There is a chance you will be given a piece of code to write.
HR Interview
  • This is the simplest round in the whole TCS NQT 2022 Hiring Process.
  • In this round , interviewer will mostly test your confidence and leadership quality.
  • They will mostly describe the Job Profile, Job Salary , and relocations in this round.
Important Note!
  1. No Negative Marking
  2. MindTree Off-Campus is Adaptive
