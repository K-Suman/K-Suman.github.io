Mention below the Steps followed by during Accenture Hiring Process 2022:-
  • Stage 1: Cognitive and Technical Assessment- Stage 1 is an elimination round.
  • Stage 2: Coding-Stage 2 is mandatory , but not elimination round in the Accenture Test Pattern.
  • Stage 3: Communication Test-Stage 3 is mandatory, nut not elimination round in the Accenture Testb Pattern.
  • Those who clear Stage 1 and attempt Stage 2 will be invited for Stage 3.
  • Stage 3's performance will be used as a reference in the interview.
  • Stage 4: Interview- Technical Interview and HR Interview.
