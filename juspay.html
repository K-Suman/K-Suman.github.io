Latest Pattern for the Juspay Recruitment Process for Freshers is given below-:-

Juspay Recruitment Process has 3-5 Rounds of Selection Process:

  • Aptitude Round
  • Online Coding Round
  • Technical Round 1
  • Technical Round 2
  • HR Round

Detailed Hiring Process of Juspay for Freshers:-
Round 1- Written Test

Folloring are the sections that are asked in Juspay Placement Exam Round 1:

  • General Aptitude Test
  • Verbal Ability Test
  • Technical Test
Round 2- Online Coding Test

The students who clear the aptitude round are called for coding round. To clear this round you should be strong with data structures and algorithms. You should be prepared Stack, queue, Tree, and Graph along with code optimization.

Round 3 - Technical Rounds

The students who clear the coding round are called for Technical Interview.To clear this round you should be clear with your basics. You should be prepared with Data structures and Algorithms, DBMS, and OOPs concepts. Students will be expected to write codes in the interview. You could be expected to optimize your previous round codes. You can expect questions from resume. You may be asked puzzles in this round.

Round 4- HR Round

Sometimes they only ask HR questions like :

  • Tell me about Yourself
  • Why JUSPAY ?
  • How you see yourself after five years from now ?
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses ?
  • Questions form resume.
