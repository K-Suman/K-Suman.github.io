Mention below the Steps followed by during Deloitte Hiring Process 2022:-
  • Round 1: Written Test- This round is the elimination round . after clearing the basic witten test , you have to go through the next round i.e. Round 2
  • Round 2: Deloitte SVAR also known as Versant Test-After clearing Round 1, Versant Test will be an automated call, and this test will be checking you up on your Speaking and Listening Skills.
  • Round 3: Interview- And in the end, you have to go through the Technical and HR Interview for the final selection.

Deloitte Off Campus Placement Test Patter Overview :-
Round 1 : Written Test

Following are the sections that are asked in Deloitte Placement Exam Round 1:

  • Quants
  • English
  • Logical Reasoning
Round 2:Versant Test (SVAR)

This is the 2nd Round of Delloite Selection Process,those who cleared the 1st Round are applicable for this round.

Versant Test's main work is to test the student's speaking skills and listening skills.

Note :If you will clear Round 2 then only you will be eligible to give an Interview .

Round 3 : Technical + HR Interview
  • This is the most difficult stage of the hiring process, so prepare as much as you can for this round.
  • Your technical knowledge , biography , and main project will be the focus of the whole interview process.
  • In this round , interviewer will mostly test your confidence and leadership quality.
  • They will mostly describe the Job Profile, Job Salary , and relocations in this round.
Important Note!
  1. No Negative Marking
  2. Cognizant Off-Campus is Adaptive
